Our investigation into the "0xchilli" entity continues. To build a complete profile of their online presence, we need to establish a timeline of their activity. Your next task is to trace their origins back to one of their primary communication platforms.

We need you to identify the exact timestamp of when the account with the username **"0xchilli"** created his Discord account. Precision is key for this forensic timeline.

## Objective

## Flag Format
The flag must be submitted in the following format (including the date and time in 12-hour format):
`GIUCTF{DD/MM/YYYY_hh:mm_AM/PM}`

**Example:** `GIUCTF{01/01/2024_03:45_PM}`
