# OSINT Challenge: Head Hunting

## Challenge Description

A simple task for you: Identify the creator of a website named **webapphackademy** and provide the official website of their current employer.

## Objective
* Find the individual who created **webapphackademy**.
* Locate the **official website** of the company or organization where they are currently employed.

## Flag Format
The flag should be submitted as the URL of the employer's website.
**Format:** `GIUCTF{website.smth}`

**Example:** `GIUCTF{google.com}`
